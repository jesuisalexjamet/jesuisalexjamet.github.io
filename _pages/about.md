I am **Alexandre V. Jamet**, a post-doctoral researcher with the SONAR Group at the [Barcelona Supercomputing Center](https://bsc.es). I completed my PhD in Computer Architecture at the [Barcelona Supercomputing Center](https://bsc.es) and [Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC)](https://www.upc.edu).

My academic focus is on General Purpose CPUs' micro-architecture, spanning from front-end to back-end design ideas.
